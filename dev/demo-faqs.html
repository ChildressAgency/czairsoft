THE BASICS

Where is CZ Located?

CZ Airsoft's main field is located at 29268 Ratliff Rd, Rhoadesville VA

When is CZ open to play?

We operate once or twice a month for both Saturday and Sunday each month. All events are posted here on our website and our other media streams (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram).

We play year round from 9am - 5pm Saturday and 1030pm - 5pm on Sunday rain or shine, year round. Changes to an event will be posted on FACEBOOK the morning of the event if there are any changes. Special Pick Up games are announced on Facebook and CZ Members receive private invitations for select events.

CZ also offers an opportunity for players to travel to other fields as a team member or guest of our CZ-Phoenix team.

What are the age restrictions to playing at CZ?

Players must be 11 years of age or older to play on their own. They can play younger as long as they have guardian supervision and have been approved bya field official.

Is CZ open to anyone or is a private invite only?

CZ is here for everyone and will always welcome walk-ons.

Everybody of every age, race, sex, creed and background is invited to participate and play with us.

All players must sign a waiver and abide by the field rules adn stipulations. (We even have our own girls team).

What should I wear for protection and are there regulations?

All players must wear proper safety equipment. FULL SEAL eye protection, face masks ro enclosed helmets only. No Mesh or Shooting glasses for anyone. (Proper Eye Protection and Helmets are available for rent or purchase).

If you don't have the gear CZ has it to rent and can even show you how and where to get the best prices to get your own.

For clothing it is highly recommended to have gloves and boots (or good shoes). If you are over the age of 18 a face mask is not mandatory but strongly suggested to avoid mouth and nose injuries.

How many players come out to your events at CZ?

We get asked this question so much and it's one of the hardest questions to address to give anyone the most direct answer. CZ has over 500 members on our Facebook page and depending on the weather, events occurring around us, traffic and at times the setting of the moon it's really hard to say. We have played with as little as 8 and as many as 130 players. No matter how many come out we are going to have a great day. On average if you just have to have an answer is to say we average 30-50 players on a perfect day.

Do you play all year round at CZ?

We play all year. 12 month strong.

We will play in just about any weather condition. Here is our general rule: Unless it is a direct downpour lightning, hail, heavy snow, ice, over 101 degrees or under 10 degrees we are going to be out there. If you, the player, wants to get out there we are going to try and make that happen.

If for some reason we have to call a day it will be done through the Facebook event with a warning the night before and a decision 2 hours before check in time. This is also why you should sign up for our Facebook site to keep updated on all the latest news.

Last but not least... EVERYONE is to have fun. If you're not tired at the end of the day, you didn't do it right.
